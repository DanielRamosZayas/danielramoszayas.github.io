Burgers and Beans

History Of A Nicaraguan Delicacy




Here in Nicaragua we have an abundance of two ingredients. Beans and Cow Meat. Our ancient ancestors discovered the art of combining the two to make nutritious meals. Tribes deep in the jungle still worship the Baked Bean God and the Prime Rib God. They say one day it will rain Burgers and Beans for all to enjoy.
